# Prologue
Now that we have answered the problems of:
- Isolation of Tenants
- Predictiblity in Deployment, Feature Releases
- Uniformity of Architecture


We move forward to the actual 🐘 elephant in the room which was hinted upon in the last post, 

#### How do We Actually drive movement from Sharded service to Cell based Service.
Remember in the last blog I warned you regarding the reason to Cell based architecture should be actual usecase.
We will assume that a `Specific Compliance is that reason` which is to be applied withing a Specific country.

This might bring the ire of your Product team, saying that its not the concern of theirs on how things will be compliant.
They are more focussed on:
- Future Growth areas
- Costs
- Trust & Compliance
- Resiliency, Isolation and Guranteed Peformance (as compared to same shard's noisy neighbours)


# How do we achieve Compliance
Externalizing Cell Configuration (into an Artifact Config) provides more control on having multiple compliance levels in same parameter/region.
